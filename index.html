Shared Homecare That Brings Joy

Small groups gathering for shared support, companionship, and everyday adventures. Because aging at home shouldn't mean aging alone.

Here's the Thing

Most of us want to age at home. But traditional homecare is expensive and isolating. You're stuck waiting around for someone to show up, help you with the basics, then leave. Meanwhile, loneliness quietly takes its toll—on health, on happiness, on the simple joy of being around people who get it.

How Granpods Works

It's pretty straightforward. We bring small groups together so care becomes a shared, social experience.

1
Form small pods of 6-8 people with similar needs
2
Make sure homes are safe and accessible
3
Assign two care attendants to each pod
4
Head out to cafes, parks, museums—real places
5
Provide care and support while everyone socializes
6
Everyone goes home at the end of the day

Why It Matters

More Affordable

When you share care, you share costs. It's that simple. Projected to be about half the price of traditional homecare.

Actually Social

Care doesn't have to be lonely. With Granpods, you're part of a group—getting out, staying connected, living life.

Better for Everyone

By pooling resources, we free up caregivers for those who truly need one-on-one support. It's a smarter system.
